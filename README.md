Project Title : Uber Eats Bangalore Restaurant Intelligence & Decision Support Systems

Description : Uber Eats operates a large-scale restaurant marketplace where business success depends on factors such as location strategy, pricing, cuisine mix, customer ratings, and platform features like online ordering and table booking.
The objective of this project was to analyze Uber Eats Bangalore restaurant data and build a decision support system that answers critical business questions using Python and SQL, presenting results as clean tabular DataFrame outputs in Streamlit.
This approach mirrors real internal analytics dashboards, where stakeholders often require precise tabular insights rather than visual storytelling.

Set-up : Python, Pandas, MySQL, Streamlit.

Business Use Cases : 
          Location Intelligence
          Partner Onboarding Strategy
          Pricing Optimization
          Cuisine Performance Analysis
          Product Feature Impact Assessment
          Market Segmentation
          Customer Satisfaction Drivers
          Expansion & Premium Restaurant Planning
